About this event

Join us for our GDG AI for Science event in Korea, where we will explore the crucial role of AI in scientific research!

Date: July 11th (Sat) 13:00 -17:00

Venue: Google Korea Office 21F

Agenda:

  • 13:00 - 13:20 Opening

  • 13:20 - 14:20 The Role of Humans in the AI Era | Prof. JiEung Kim, Yonsei University

  • 14:20 - 14:30 Break

  • 14:30 - 14:50 AI for Science: Accelerating Research Breakthroughs | Heiga Zen, Google DeepMind

  • 14:50 - 15:40 Gemma in the Lab | Juyeong Ji, Google DeepMind

  • 15:40 - 15:50 Break

  • 15:50 - 16:50 Shift Happens: Robustness and Reliability of Multimodal Foundation Models | Naman Goyal, Google DeepMind & Jenny Ni, Google

  • 16:50 - 17:00 Closing

Connect with experts and fellow researchers, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and discover how AI is shaping the future of science. Don't miss this exciting opportunity to be part of a community dedicated to advancing AI-driven scientific innovation.
